ABOUT US

Campus Connect is a web app that helps students find academic support, build friendships, and get more involved on campus. Create a profile with your major, skills, interests, and availability, then match with tutors, study partners, groups, and events that fit your goals.


In one place, you can search tutoring offers and requests, RSVP to events, join groups, message classmates, and explore a skill-exchange marketplace for languages, music, fitness, and more. Campus Connect is designed to make campus feel more connected, supportive, and easier to navigate for every student.
