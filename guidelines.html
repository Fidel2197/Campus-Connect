Community Guidelines & Reporting

Learn how to participate respectfully and report any issues.

Key Guidelines

Campus Connect is built to help students learn, collaborate, and connect. To keep everyone safe and supported, please follow these core expectations:

  • Be respectful & professional

    Use clear, courteous language in messages and posts. Treat classmates, tutors, and organizers the way you’d want to be treated.



  • No spam, harassment, or discrimination

    Do not send repeated unwanted messages, advertise irrelevant services, or target anyone based on identity, background, or beliefs.



  • Be honest about your skills & availability

    Accurately represent your experience, rates, and schedule so other students can rely on the information you share.



  • Keep conversations on-topic & safe

    Use Campus Connect for academic, professional, and campus-related networking. Report anything that feels unsafe, threatening, or clearly outside these goals.

Report Content

If you see something that breaks these guidelines, you can submit a report below. A moderator or admin will review the details and follow up as needed.

What happens next?

  • We review the report based on these community guidelines.
  • We may contact you through the app if we need more detail.
  • Appropriate action is taken (warning, content removal, or account limits).
Immediate safety concern?

For emergencies or situations where someone may be in danger, contact your campus safety office or local emergency services first, then submit a report here if needed.
